Play on the Official Kurgan Server

Would you like to experience Kurgan MUD without setting up your own server? You're welcome to join our public server and start your adventure right away!

The Kurgan MUD server is always online and open to all players. All you need is a MUD client or a simple terminal connection.

  • Host: 37.48.78.239
  • Port: 4000

To connect using telnet from your terminal, simply run: 

telnet 37.48.78.239 4000

If you prefer a graphical MUD client, we recommend:

  • Mudlet (cross-platform, modern UI)
  • CMUD (Windows only, advanced scripting)
  • TinTin++ (powerful command-line client)
